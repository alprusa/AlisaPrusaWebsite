WALKING THROUGH HOMELESSNESS

ABOUT


Walking Through Homelessness is an exploration in the causes and effects of homelessness in an approachable, interactable experience. All Quotes are taken from homeless people in intervies by Steven VanderStaay in Street Lives: An Oral History of Homeless Americans. This project was developed by students of UCSC Film 177, to act as a more personal, individual account of modern-day homelessness.
