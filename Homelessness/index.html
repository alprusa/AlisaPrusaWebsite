WALKING THROUGH HOMELESSNESS

HOME


Walking Through Homelessness is an interactive experience
that aims to allow you to walk through the streets, meeting
and interacting with homeless individuals and hearing what they
have to say about their lifestyle.

In order to interact, use the LEFT and RIGHT
arrow keys to move your avatar, and press
SPACEBAR or LEFT-CLICK to interact with various
points of interest.
